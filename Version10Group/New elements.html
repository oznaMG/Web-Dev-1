New Elements

< aside >

Definition:

The <aside> tag is used for extra information that is related to the main content but not the main focus.

When to Use It:

  • For sidebars with extra details
  • For ads or promotions when it's related to the main thing.
  • For quotes or links to related articles.
  • For notes, reminders, or related resources.

Example

< blockquote >

The <blockquote> tag is used to show a quote or something someone else said.

  • When you want to show a long quote from a person or a book.
  • When you need to reference something said by someone else.

Example

< cite >

Definition:

The <cite> is used to mark up the title of a cited creative work.

When to use it:

  • To reference the title of a book, article, website, or other creative work.
  • To give credit to the source of the information you're quoting or discussing.
