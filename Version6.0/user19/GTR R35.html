GTR 35

The Nissan GT-R R35, introduced in 2007, made an immediate impact on motorsports with its cutting-edge technology and raw performance. It quickly became a force in the FIA GT Championship, Super GT, and various other racing series, where its 3.8-liter twin-turbo V6 engine and sophisticated all-wheel-drive system gave it a competitive edge. The R35’s advanced traction and handling systems, like the ATTESA E-TS Pro and Super-HICAS, allowed it to excel in endurance races and sprint events alike. It earned notable victories in global endurance racing, including at events like the 24 Hours of Nürburgring, where it proved its durability and speed. With its combination of real-world racing success and cutting-edge performance, the R35 solidified the GT-R's legacy as one of the most formidable machines in motorsport.
